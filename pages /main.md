## Welcome to Project Nexium

**Project Nexium** is a secret organization dedicated to the containment and study of anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena that violate natural law.

Our mission is to **secure** these anomalies, ocassionaly **contain** them, and **protect** humanity from their effects.
